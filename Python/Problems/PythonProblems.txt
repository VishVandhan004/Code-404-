9th Problem
Given time in seconds, prints the time in days:hours:minutes:seconds
Example
input=25421
output=0:7:3:41
input=95467
output=1:2:31:7
input=86400
output=1:0:0:0

def time(sec):
    days =sec//86400
    hr=(sec % 86400)//3600
    min=((sec % 86400)%3600)//60
    sec=((sec%86400)%3600)%60
    print(f"{days} :{hr}: {min}: {sec}")
    
n=int(input())
time(n)
